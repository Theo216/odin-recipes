Tuna salad recipe

Description

The following is a quick and healthy tuna salad recipe

Ingredients:

  • canned tuna.
  • mayonnaise.
  • celery.
  • red onion.
  • parsley.
  • half a tablespoon of Dijon mustard .
  • salt and pepper, to taste.

Cooking instructions:

  1. Drain the tuna.
