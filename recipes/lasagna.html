Lasagna recipe

Description:

The following is a homemade lasagna recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground pork.
  • 500g lean ground beef.
  • 500g minced onion.
  • 150g crushed tomatoes.
  • 100g tomato sauch.
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley.
  • 1 clove.
  • 1 and a half teaspoon salt.
  • 1 and a half teaspoon dried basil.
  • half a teaspoon dried oregano.
  • quarter teaspoon sugar.
  • 200g package of lasagna noodles.
  • 500g small-curd cottage cheese.
  • three quarter cup of grated Parmesan cheese.
  • 3 large eggs.
  • 2 teaspoons salt.
  • 1 quarter teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese.

Cooking instructions:

  1. Combine the pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally for 30 minutes.
  3. While sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bit, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  4. While the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 190 degrees C.
  5. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  6. Assemble lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to coat it; Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover. Layer with half of the cheese mixture, half of the remaining sauce, and half of the mozzarella cheese; Repeat layers once more using the remaining noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozzarella, then cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Bake in the preheated over for 30 to 40 minutes; Then, remove the foil and bake until the cheese is golden brown, approximately 5 to 10 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
