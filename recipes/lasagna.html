Lasagna recipe

Description

The following is a homemade lasagna recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground pork
  • 500g lean ground beef
  • 500g minced onion
  • 150g crushed tomatoes
  • 100g tomato sauch
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1 clove
  • 1 and a half teaspoon salt
  • 1 and a half teaspoon dried basil
  • half a teaspoon dried oregano
  • quarter teaspoon sugar
  • 200g package of lasagna noodles
  • 500g small-curd cottage cheese
  • three quarter cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • quarter teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Combine the pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally for 30 minutes.
  3. While sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bit, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
